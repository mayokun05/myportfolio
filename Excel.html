An Interactive Excel Dashboard on Sales Dataset

Dive into an in-depth analysis of sales performance through this interactive dashboard.
By examining key metrics such as total revenue, total price, and total quantity, we can gain a comprehensive understanding of sales dynamics.

Recommendation:
- Use insights to optimize sales strategies by focusing on high-performing items and top-contributing states.
- Monitor daily and monthly revenue trends to identify peak periods and adjust inventory and staffing accordingly.

Project Title

This project is a visualization of transaction data from OList. OList is the largest department store in Brazil. The dataset has information on 90k orders from 2016 to 2018 made at multiple marketplaces in Brazil.
